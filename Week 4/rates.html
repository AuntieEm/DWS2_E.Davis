Vacation Pet Sitting

For pet owners on vacation.

Scheduled according to Auntie Em's daily schedule. 7 days/week

All visits are completed within a 2 hour time window.

Auntie Em does not accept specific appointments.

Services Can Include:

  • Feed pets their meals
  • Refresh their water
  • Dog Walks
  • Administer medications if needed
  • Clean litter box or cage
  • Bring in mail, newspaper, and packages
  • Take out trash and recycling or bring back in
  • Water plants (minimal) alternate lights and blinds
  • Give pets plenty of TLC (belly rubs, head scratches, etc)

*Holiday fees may apply.
