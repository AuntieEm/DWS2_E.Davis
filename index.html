Our Mission

Here at Auntie Em's Inc, we do more than just house or pet sitting since 2009. We take care of the things most important to you when you are away. We keep your home safe and looking lived in while also taking care of your pets. We keep them happy and healthy while you are away so you can spend your time worry-free. We promise to love your pets as much as you do and treat them as our own. We believe that you and your pets will be happier with a live-in sitter rather than being lonely and stressed out in a pet-boarding facility. We are certified by both the National Association of Professional Pet sitters and Pet Sitters International. We are bonded and insured through Pet Sitters Associates.
